Role models are a large part of what makes you, you. A role model is someone you admire so much, your choices and decisions have them factored in. Who we find to be our role model then has a lot to say about what you value, and who you are as a person.

The heroes that I most admire have the following characteristics.

  • Courageous - they stand up for what they belive is right and lead the way
  • Motivate others - people follow a great leader
  • Great kindness - widely known to have a caring heart
  • Hard working - they set lofty goals and move forward on that path
  • Integrity - they live true to their calling

Someone who fully embodies these characteristics to me is Hannah Erickson. Known by many on the University of Northern Colorado campus, she always is a hard worker and demonstrates the definition of kindness to everyone. She is an example of someone who works hard to achieve their goals, will always be a helping hand, and stands up for what she believes is right.
